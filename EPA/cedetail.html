Civil Engineering – BTech / Diploma

This Civil Engineering course builds a strong foundation in construction technology, surveying, structural design, and material science. Students learn how roads, bridges, buildings, and water systems are planned and executed.

You will gain practical knowledge of RCC design, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, environmental engineering, and construction planning with real-world projects.

After completing this program, you can work in construction firms, government departments, real estate companies, and infrastructure industries.
