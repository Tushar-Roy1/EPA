SolidWorks – 3D Modeling & Product Design

The SolidWorks course at EPA is designed for students who want to gain strong skills in 3D CAD modeling and mechanical design. This is ideal for Mechanical, Automobile, Manufacturing, and Production engineering students.

You will learn sketching, part modeling, assembly creation, sheet metal, surface modeling, engineering drawing, and simulation tools used in industries.

After completing this course, students can work as CAD Engineers, Product Designers, Mechanical Designers, and R&D Assistants.
